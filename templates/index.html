File sharing applications are software tools designed to allow users to share files with others, either within a local network or over the internet.

User Authentication and Authorization

User registration and login.Role-based access control to restrict access to files and folders.

File Upload and Download

The ability to upload files to the application. Download files from the application to local devices. Generates unique file identifiers or links

Account Creation

Account creation is the initial step in establishing your online presence.

Password must contains

  • At least 12 characters length
  • At least 1 number (0...9)
  • At least 1 lowercase letter (a...z)
  • At least 1 special symbol (!...$)
  • At least 1 uppercase letter (A...Z)
